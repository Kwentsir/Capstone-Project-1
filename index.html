Ghana Tech
Summit
2022

It is with great pleasure that we invite you to attend the 4th Annual Ghana Tech Summit. Ghana Tech Summit brings together hundreds of entrepreneurs, investors, digital marketers and creatives under one roof together to address humanity’s greatest challenges via technology and entrepreneurship.

2022.06.1(WED) ~ 2022.12.30(FRI)

@ Free Virtual program

Main Program

Lecture

This free live summit event will welcome hundreds of digital delegates from across Ghana and around the world to discuss the future of employment, entrepreneurship, and education in Ghana as the country faces its highest level of youth unemployment.

Exhibition

Participants will have an opportunity to showcase their digital products and inventions to entrepreneurs, investors, digital marketers and creatives.

Forum

Participants will have an opportunity to discuss the future of technology in Africa with entrepreneurs, investors, digital marketers and creatives.

Workshop

Insights and initiatives leveraging tech and innovative inclusive ways to accelerate the future of Ghana will be discussed.

Networking

The 2022 edition is seeking to welcome over 10000 global digital delegates. There are opportunities to network with tech industry gurus accross the world. Networking is the masterstroke when it comes to marketing your business, and nothing can beat connecting with your audience personally.
